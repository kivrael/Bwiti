About Our Center

Welcome to Sacred Forest, a sanctuary dedicated to preserving and sharing the ancient wisdom of the Bwiti tradition. Nestled in the sacred forests of Gabon, our center offers authentic spiritual experiences rooted in thousands of years of ancestral knowledge.

Our mission is to guide seekers on transformative journeys of healing and self-discovery through traditional ceremonies, sacred plant medicine, and the profound teachings of Bwiti spirituality.

Every ritual, every teaching, and every moment at Sacred Forest is conducted with deep respect for the traditions passed down through generations of Bwiti initiates and healers.

Our Master

Master Missengue

Master Missengue is a revered guardian of ancient Bwiti wisdom, deeply initiated in the sacred mysteries of the tradition. With decades of experience guiding souls through spiritual transformation, he carries the sacred knowledge passed down through generations of Bwiti masters.

Through his guidance, countless individuals have discovered their true path, healed deep wounds, and connected with the profound wisdom of the ancestors. His teachings encompass not only the ceremonial aspects of Bwiti but also the practical wisdom for living in harmony with nature and spirit.

Every ceremony and teaching session is infused with his deep compassion, authentic knowledge, and unwavering commitment to the sacred traditions.
